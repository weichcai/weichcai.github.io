Biography

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in School of Electronics and Information Technology, Sun Yat-sen University, advised by Ming Li (Prof. Li just transfered to Duke Kunshan University from Sun Yat-sen University recently).

My research interests mainly focus on automatic speaker and language recognition, particularly towards deep learning based end-to-end approach. Since 2014, I have been studying in the area of speech processing in the lab of Speech and Multimodal Intelligent Information Processing (SMIIP) directed by Prof. Li.
